2.  **Understand RSI (Relative Strength Index):**
    *   Momentum oscillator that measures the speed and change of price movements.
    *   Scales from 0 to 100.
    *   Traditionally, RSI > 70 is overbought (suracheté), RSI < 30 is oversold (survendu).
    *   In crypto, it helps identify potential trend reversals or continuations.
